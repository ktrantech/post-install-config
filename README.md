<h1>Tails OS - Opening Tails on your USB Flash drive</h1>
This tutorial outlines opening Tails using your USB Flash Drive to accomplish ultimate protection against surveillance and censorship  .<br />


<h2>Environments and Technologies Used</h2>

- Lenovo Think Pad
- MacBook Air
- USB Flash Drive
  
<h2>Operating Systems Used </h2>

- Windows 10</b> (21H2)
- macOS 


<h2>Configuration Steps</h2>

Step 1. Shut Down your Computer 

<img width="1034" alt="Screenshot 2025-02-10 at 8 15 31 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/61462ac8-8195-483e-92c9-8392de2f514c" />


  
<h2>Step 2. Restart on your Tails USB stick
</h2>

- Make sure your Flash Drive is Plugged in
- Then Restart your Computer

  <img width="637" alt="Screenshot 2025-02-10 at 8 17 11 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/8b8404e3-9b6c-4608-8443-5a1d42c53aa5" />

 - Click on the Start button
 - Press and hold the Shift key while you choose Power ▸ Restart
 - In the Choose an option screen, choose Use a device


   <h2>Step 3. Boot Menu on Computer Rebooting </h2>

- The boot menu will look something like this ⬇️

  <img width="413" alt="Screenshot 2025-02-10 at 8 17 51 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/0a01fac1-f3a6-4525-bbde-f311d05d0131" />

- In the Boot Menu, select your USB stick and press Enter
- If the computer starts on Tails, the Boot Loader appears and Tails starts automatically after 4 seconds


   
   <h2>Step 4. Welcome to Tails!</h2>

<img width="390" alt="Screenshot 2025-02-10 at 11 42 42 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/8d9b05ee-cf5d-4f8d-b57a-59bc550d9ae9" />

- After the Boot Menu, a loading screen appears like this ⬇️

<img width="689" alt="Screenshot 2025-02-10 at 11 42 52 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/efc97f84-5d08-45d4-aee5-5459f43dc1ac" />


- One to two minutes after the Boot Loader and the loading screen, the Welcome Screen appears
- The welcome screen will look something like this ⬇️

  <img width="692" alt="Screenshot 2025-02-10 at 8 19 26 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/0a0a7188-fa61-41db-bc29-23dac76f251b" />

- In the Welcome Screen, select your language and keyboard layout in the Language and Formats section.
- Click Start Tails
- After 15–30 seconds, the Tails desktop appears
